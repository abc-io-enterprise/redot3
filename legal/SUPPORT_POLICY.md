## 3. Support Hours

### Human Support

- **Days:** Monday through Friday
- **Hours:** 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern Standard Time (EST)
- **Exclusions:** Weekends and recognized United States federal holidays

### Autonomous / AI Support

- **Availability:** 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year
- **Scope:** Automated diagnostics, status checks, routing of emergency beacon traffic, and basic troubleshooting guidance.

## 4. Response Times

Response times are targets and may vary based on issue severity and volume:

| Severity | Description                                                         | Target Response         |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Critical | Service-wide outage, security incident, or emergency beacon failure | Within 1 hour, 24/7     |
| High     | Major feature impairment affecting multiple users                   | Within 4 business hours |
| Medium   | Partial feature issue or single-user problem                        | Within 1 business day   |
| Low      | General questions, feature requests, or minor issues                | Within 2 business days  |

## 5. Scope of Support

ABC-IO provides support for:

- Service availability and uptime
- Account access and authentication issues
- Billing and subscription questions
- Bug reports and feature requests
- API usage guidance
- Emergency beacon and public safety features

Support does not include:

- Custom development or integration work outside documented APIs
- Third-party services not operated by ABC-IO
- End-user devices, networks, or local infrastructure

## 6. Emergency and Beacon Support

Emergency beacon and public safety features are monitored continuously by autonomous systems. Human escalation is available for verified critical incidents. Misuse of emergency features may result in suspension.

## 7. Service Level Objective

ABC-IO targets high availability for production services. Scheduled maintenance will be announced in advance when possible. The Service may experience brief interruptions during updates.

## 8. Support Limitations

- Support is provided based on the subscription tier or service agreement in effect.
- Response times are targets, not guarantees.
- We reserve the right to limit support for accounts in violation of the Terms of Service or Acceptable Use Policy.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Support Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted at https://abc-io.com/support-policy with a revised effective date.
