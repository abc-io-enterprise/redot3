## 1. General Policy

ABC-IO aims to provide fair and transparent billing. This Refund Policy explains when refunds may be issued for payments made through the Service.

## 2. Subscription Services

- Subscriptions are billed in advance based on the selected billing cycle.
- You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting support.
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
- Unless otherwise required by law, fees already paid are non-refundable.

## 3. Eligibility for Refunds

A refund may be considered in the following situations:

- Duplicate charges or billing errors caused by ABC-IO
- Failure to deliver the Service due to a prolonged outage attributable to ABC-IO
- Cancellation within any applicable statutory cooling-off period required by law
- Other exceptional circumstances determined at the sole discretion of ABC-IO

## 4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally not eligible for refund:

- Fees for partially used subscription periods
- Setup, onboarding, or professional service fees
- One-time purchases or add-ons after delivery
- Charges resulting from account misuse or violation of the Terms of Service

## 5. Refund Process

To request a refund:

1. Contact support@abc-io.com with your account information and reason for the request.
2. Include transaction details, date of charge, and any relevant documentation.
3. ABC-IO will review the request and respond within 10 business days.

Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on the payment processor.

## 6. Chargebacks and Disputes

We encourage users to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback or payment dispute. Unjustified chargebacks may result in account suspension.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted at https://abc-io.com/refund-policy with a revised effective date.
