Free Beacon

A free, anonymous public safety service. Share your location to receive safety alerts, weather, nearby events, and suggestions. No account required.

Launch Beacon PWA Community Hub

How It Works

  • Open the Beacon PWA on any device — no app store needed
  • Tap "Share My Location & Get Info" to opt in
  • Receive weather, public safety alerts, nearby suggestions, and public events
  • Your location is used only for this response and is never stored
  • Optional: emit an emergency/transit/SOS beacon only if you need help
  • All beacons expire after 24 hours

Privacy Promise

  • No account required
  • No personal information collected
  • Location is used only to generate your response
  • No persistent device identifiers
  • All data auto-expires
