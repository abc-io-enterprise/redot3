What You Get

Our communications provider service turns any phone, tablet, desktop, wearable, or embedded device into a secure node on the ABC-IO mesh. Single users, families, and engineering teams can send interface-to-interface messages, translate across sensory modalities, relay beacons, and stay connected even when primary infrastructure is unavailable.
