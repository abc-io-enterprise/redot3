📡 Free Beacon & Awareness PWA

The ABC-IO Beacon is a free, installable web app that shares public events, weather, and important local information when you choose to share your location. We do not track you. Your location is only used at the moment you submit it to return relevant public information.

  1. Open https://abc-io.com/beacon/ on your phone or computer.
  2. Tap Share Location if you want local awareness info.
  3. Tap your browser menu and choose Add to Home Screen to install the PWA.
  4. Re-submit your location anytime to refresh the public information.

🔐 Account Interface PWA

For customers and team members: the ABC-IO Account Interface is a private, installable PWA with messaging, product management, billing, and quick access to translation and beacon services.

  1. Sign up or log in at abc-io.com/login.html.
  2. Open abc-io.com/account/ in your mobile browser.
  3. Tap your browser menu and choose Add to Home Screen to install the PWA.
  4. Message other users on your account, manage add-ons, and access help from one place.
