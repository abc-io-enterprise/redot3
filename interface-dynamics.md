## The 25 Frontend Interface Types## 1. Visual Input Interfaces

- V → V (Visual to Visual Mirror): High-density canvas rendering engine for scaling, color-correcting, and spatial-mapping raw visual feeds.
- V → A (Sonification Interface): Interactive spatial matrix that converts image histograms, color spectrums, and movement vectors into synthesized audio tones or speech descriptions.
- V → H (Optical-to-Haptic Map): Depth-mapping dashboard that translates image textures and edges into vibrational frequency controls for haptic hardware.
- V → O (Chromatographic Scent Renderer): AI-driven color-to-aroma UI mapping specific visual wavelengths or objects to trigger localized olfactory diffusers.
- V → G (Visual Flavor Synthesizer): Calibration portal correlating visual presentation variables (color intensity, shapes) into micro-dosed chemical taste commands.

## 2. Auditory Input Interfaces

- A → V (Visualizer Dashboard): Dynamic oscilloscopes, spectrograms, and particle emission layers mapping audio pitch, frequency, and cadence to geometric art.
- A → A (Acoustic Equalization Hub): Real-time spatial audio directional canvas for filtering, cleaning, and mirroring audio data paths.
- A → H (Audio-Tactile Sub-Woofer UI): Bass and rhythm frequency router translating low-to-high decibel tracks into distinct rhythmic vibrations on wearable tech.
- A → O (Acoustic-Scent Atmosphere Trigger): Ambient soundscape decoder matching environmental audio triggers (e.g., forest sounds) to aromatic micro-burst schedules.
- A → G (Sonic Seasoning Panel): Crossmodal frequency controller applying high/low pitch audio tracks shown to enhance sweet/bitter taste perception during ingestion.

## 3. Tactile/Haptic Input Interfaces

- H → V (Kinesthetic Tracking Screen): Physical pressure-map visualization showing real-time heatmaps of force, friction, and texture data inputs.
- H → A (Pressure Sonifier): Synthesizer node network converting kinetic touch velocities and material resistance into varying sound pitches and volume nodes.
- H → H (Haptic Telepresence Node): Pure low-latency websocket portal synchronizing remote input touch arrays with local haptic-pin actuator arrays.
- H → O (Texture-Aroma Bridge): Interactive controller mapping tactile friction profiles (rough, smooth) to correlating ambient scents (woodsmoke, linen).
- H → G (Mouthfeel-to-Taste Emulator): Rheology control layout matching textures to physical taste adjustments, mimicking viscosity changes via chemical signals.

## 4. Olfactory Input Interfaces

- O → V (Scent Vapor Plotter): Chemical sensor data UI visualizing molecular density, compound concentrations, and scent dispersal vectors using complex 3D particle systems.
- O → A (Olfactory Symphony Module): Pitch-mapping application linking molecular weights and specific odor groups to specific musical chords or ambient noise filters.
- O → H (Aroma-Texture Transformer): Tactile mapping grid linking specific scent groups (e.g., sharp mint) to crisp or jagged physical actuator frequencies.
- O → O (Scent Relay Dashboard): Molecular formula compositor translating electronic nose hardware inputs directly into local aroma diffuser fluid recipe mixes.
- O → G (Flavor Profile Combinator): Retronasal correlation screen balancing scent output with taste fluid releases to create complete synthetic food flavors.

## 5. Gustatory Input Interfaces

- G → V (Taste Palette Spectrum): Electronic tongue telemetry visualizer mapping 5-axis profiles (sweet, sour, salty, bitter, umami) onto a dynamic multi-colored radar chart.
- G → A (Gustatory Melody Array): Harmonic tone generator translating chemical pH and flavor compound concentrations into chords (e.g., sour maps to high-frequency dissonant tones).
- G → H (Chemical-Haptic Matrix): Neuromorphic feedback console matching chemical irritation elements (like spicy capsaicin) to physical heat or pulsing vibration cues.
- G → O (Taste-Aroma Reverse Splitter): Separation processor translating liquid taste sensor values into ambient vaporous scent cues.
- G → G (Flavor Replication Terminal): Liquid dispenser telemetry hub that duplicates taste profiles remotely by driving micro-fluidic actuators in real-time.
